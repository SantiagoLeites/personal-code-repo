List A

  1. Spread the pesto on the pizza crust as thick or thin as you like.
  2. Cover the top evenly with tomato slices.
  3. Spread the red onion (to taste) over the top and sprinkle with pine nuts.
  4. Add globs and crumbles of goat cheese randomly. (Kalamata olives are nice topping as well.)
  5. Bake in a 400 degree oven for 10 to 15 minutes until sizzling and fragant.
